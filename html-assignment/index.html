About Me

Hi! My name is Lily and I'm a recent highschool graduate pursing a design degree based in Canada. Originally, I planned on pursing a fashion business & design degree but ultimately decided on a general design degree not only for more career opportunities, but also monetary reasons. Other than my interest in fashion, I have no idea what I'd like to do for a career. I'm hoping that this degree allows me to explore and figure out what in specific I enjoy.

So far, I'm enjoying the HTML & CSS web development languages and graphic design the most. But in the end, my main career goal is to cater to my introverted self, and be able to interact with the least amount of people whilst spending most of my time infront of a computer, preferably at a home office. From what I have learned so far, it seems that coding & freelance design jobs offer this, keeping to private conversations amongst small teams or one client.
